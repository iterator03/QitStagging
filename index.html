Developed in 30 hours : Solves commmon man's problem

The idea to develop Q-It striked us when we were waiting in a very long queue in IIIT Alahabad for regsitration of the Hackathon. We thought why can't we develop something that can automatically notify the individual when their turn came rather than making a queue. That was the point when we started working on Q-It. We developed Q-It merely from an app to an analytics service as well.

Virtual Queue Allocation

Scan the QR code and Q-It automatically allocates you a queue number and you are notified when your turn comes.

Q-It analytics provides live customer analytics that include present customer count, total customer count and bounce rates.
